Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need to bring our own laptops?

For coding and creative events like Binary Blitz and Future Forge, participants are required to bring their own laptops. The school will provide desktops for the quiz events.

Is lunch provided for participants?

Yes, a complimentary lunch and refreshments will be provided to all registered participants and accompanying teachers. Details will be available at the help desk.

How are teams formed for events?

Teams must be formed prior to registration. Please enter the details for all team members in the Google Form under the appropriate event section.

What is the reporting time on event day?

All participants are requested to report to the venue by 8:30 AM IST for check-in. The opening ceremony will begin promptly at 9:00 AM.
